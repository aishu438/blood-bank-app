Password Policy

  1. Email IDs not logged in for over a month will be locked. The account will still receive emails, but login will be disabled. On request, the password will be reset and shared with the user.
  2. Invalid password entry more than 5 times will lock the account. Reset will be done and communicated on request.
  3. Users are responsible for all activities performed using their Email IDs. Do not share or use others’ credentials. Treat passwords as confidential.
  4. Change your password immediately if it's suspected to be compromised or known by an unauthorized person.
  5. Temporary passwords must be changed on the first login.
  6. Password reset is mandatory every 30 days. Failing to do so will expire the current password.
  7. Idle time logout is enabled to prevent cybersecurity threats and misuse.
  8. Password reset requests must be raised through designation-based emails (e.g., Managers, Senior Managers, Superintending Engineers) from the @tnebnet.org domain only.
  9. The owner of the user ID is fully responsible for the safety and confidentiality of credentials. Passwords must not be shared with colleagues, vendors, or clients.
  10. If an email account is found to be violated or corrupted, it will be locked without prior notice and reset on request.

❌ Don'ts

  • Do not reveal your password to anyone. The Email ID owner is solely responsible for its maintenance.
  • Do not save your passwords in any format (browser, password manager, printed copy, etc.).
  • Avoid setting weak passwords such as repeating the username.
  • Do not reuse the last three passwords when resetting.
  • Password reset requests from personal domains (e.g., Gmail, Yahoo) will not be entertained.
  • Requests from @tnebltd.org or name-based emails will not be accepted for designation-based email IDs.
