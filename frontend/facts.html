Blood Facts


  • One donation can save up to three lives.
  • Every two seconds, someone in need requires blood.
  • Type O-negative is the universal blood type used in emergencies.
  • Platelets, derived from blood, are crucial for cancer patients.
  • Blood cannot be manufactured; it can only come from volunteers.
  • The average adult has about 10 pints of blood in their body.
  • Healthy donors can give whole blood every 56 days.
