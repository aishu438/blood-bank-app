Health & Preparation Tips

Make your blood donation experience safe, smooth, and impactful.

✔️ Stay Hydrated

Drink plenty of water before and after your donation. Avoid caffeinated beverages right before donating.

✔️ Eat a Healthy Meal

Have a nutritious meal within 2 hours before your donation. Include foods rich in iron like spinach, red meat, or legumes.

✔️ Get Enough Sleep

A good night's rest ensures your body is well-prepared for blood donation and recovery.

✔️ Bring Valid ID

Carry your government-issued ID or blood donor card for registration and verification.

✔️ Relax & Wear Comfortable Clothing

Stay calm during the process and wear short sleeves or sleeves that can be rolled up easily.

✔️ Avoid Heavy Exercise Post Donation

Refrain from intense physical activity for the rest of the day to allow your body to recover.
