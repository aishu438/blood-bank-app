Donation Eligibility

Ensure you're ready and eligible to save lives through blood donation

Who Can Donate?

  • ✅ Aged between 18 to 65 years
  • ✅ Weighs more than 50kg
  • ✅ Hemoglobin level at least 12.5 g/dL
  • ✅ Overall healthy with no major illness
  • ✅ Pulse and blood pressure within normal range
  • ✅ Not pregnant or breastfeeding (for female donors)

Who Cannot Donate?

  • ❌ Has cold, flu, or active infection
  • ❌ Recently had surgery or blood transfusion
  • ❌ On antibiotics or certain medications
  • ❌ Had a tattoo/piercing within last 6 months
  • ❌ History of hepatitis, HIV/AIDS, or chronic diseases
  • ❌ Alcohol consumption within last 24 hours

Still unsure? Visit your nearest blood bank for a free eligibility checkup!
