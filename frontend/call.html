Blood on Call

In emergencies, time is life. Our Blood on Call service ensures that life-saving blood is available when and where it is needed most.

How It Works

  • Call our 24/7 hotline for emergency blood requests.
  • We dispatch the nearest available blood unit.
  • Receive blood directly at hospitals or homes (as applicable).
